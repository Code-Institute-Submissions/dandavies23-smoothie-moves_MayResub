Smoothie Moves

A fruity-fun memory game for children over 4

Start
How to play
  • The aim of the game is to make a healthy smoothie using fruit and vegetables hiding behind the cups
  • Match the fruit and vegetables to complete the pairs, making a healthy, delicious smoothie
  • Collect extra points by pairing up ingredients in as few moves as possible in the quickest time
  • Are you a beginner blueberry, a better broccoli, a cool carrot or a brilliant beetroot?
